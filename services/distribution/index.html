Film Distribution

Distribution is the toughest part of selling any film. FD editors specialize in film, TV, documentary, PSAs, commercials, VFX and CGI. By utilizing industry standard suites such as Avid Suite (Media Composer, Symphony, Pro Tools) and the Adobe Creative Suite (Premiere, After Effects, Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash). FD Productions has made a name for itself within the post-production space. FD's post production services include offline editing, online editing, color correction, titling, creating digital assets (graphics), 2D and 3D animation, sound recording and engineering, music composition and scoring, and BluRay DVD authoring.
