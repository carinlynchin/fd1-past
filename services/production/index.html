  • Principal Photography
    Lights. Camera. Action!

    Assuming a feature or narrative is being produced, thisphase of film production is generally the most expensive because of the large amount of labor and rent expense including: actor, director, crew salaries, art salaries, hair/make-up, camera equipment, props, and on-set special effects. All parties must be in open communications and strive to remain on time and on budget.

The FD crew works directly with talent and rents major motion picture film equipment that are perfectly suited to your project and within budget. FD offers the following production services: ENG camera operators, field and studio production, interviews, broll, renactments, directing, cinematography, folly sound, production lighting, mixers, boom operators, set design, hair and make up coordination, Arri Alexa LF Cameras, Arri Skypanels, and all necessary insurances for our crew to operate. FD's management believes renting production gear almost always ensures higher quality satisfaction to the specific needs of FD clients because every camera is different. The ideal camera for one production may be several different ones and/or may change depending on client specifications. When using extremely high caliber equipment a still or digital image can be easily used as a standalone for any purpose and clients love having the ability to export stills from the acquired media.
