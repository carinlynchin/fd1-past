Production Information

Production, of any type commercial, narrative, or promotional in nature, consists of developing a treatment (script) to create a blueprint as a proxy to the detailed pre-production process. FD's creative directors focus on crucial project elements such as defining ideal locations, optimizing lighting to set mood and annunciate the story we are trying to tell, set design, wardrobe, hair and make-up, music and overall dynamic visual style. Upon engaging FD Prouductions, our teams storyboards the concept with an extreme obsession over details, and no production occurs until we make certain all preperation is 100% to execute the narrative storytelling concept. Video and marketing go together like peanut butter and jelly. From traditional television commercials its no secret that video has become a staple of our everyday lives. While convenient and efficient for the consumer, video marketing provides marketers with an attractive, versatile, and extremely shareable medium to reach their audiences. In fact, Diode Digital recently found that online video is a 600% more effective marketing tool than print and direct mail combined. FD Productions specializes in pre-production, production, and post-production cinematography services to a variety of industries. The Company has thrived on developing cinematic, meaningful, motion pictures with excellent professionalism to our clients. Armed with some of the most most state of the art cinema-grade camera stabilization systems, FD Productions is truly a one-stop shop for all production needs shooting at resolutions up to 6K with or without aerial cinematography platforms to depict some of the most breath-taking establishing shots.
