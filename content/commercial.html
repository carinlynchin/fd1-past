FD Snapshot
FD Productions specializes in providing full professional video production services and aerial cinematography services for motion picture, television, and corporate use. Fully insured and FAA Section 333 Exempt providing aerial cinematography services.

Our professionals specialize in full ENG production (max camera crew size of 12, including best boy, grip, etc.), Editing Services Avid | Premiere CC, Storyboard Artists, Script Editors, Cinema Drone Filming and Rental Services, and Gimbal Services.

In past, we've been fortunate enough to provide camera operators (ground or aerial) for several dozen features, television series, pilots, and Fortune 500 Companies including NBC, American Eagle Outfitters, Porsche, Autonation, Google, YouTube, Teles Properties, OnTrac, Acura. We've provided these services mainly in the following capacities Producer, Director, D.P., Camera Operator, Drone Pilot, or editor.

Our team is led by Shaun Khan who mainly provides director and director of photography and editing services with a specialized background in ENG/ EFP Production (director, d.p., 1st a.d., aerial unit coordinator, drone pilot, and drone/ronin camera operator.
