FD Productions is a motion picture company specializing in the production of aerial media; both videography and photography. FD caters to the needs of luxury real estate firms, commercial real estate firms, construction companies, cities and municipalities, and businesses. FD Productions differentiates itself by providing aerial footage including the interior and exterior of the business premise and capturing a clear perspective of the most relevant aspects of our client's subject matter.
