Portfolio

FD provides film and video production for motion picture, television, and corporate use. Fully insured and FAA Section 333 Exempt providing aerial cinematography services. Our professionals specialize in pre-production, production, and/or post-production. Our creative directors can provide full campaigns and provide full ENG production (max camera crew size of 12, including best boy, grip, etc.), editing services, storyboard artists, script editors, drone filming, distribution/concept planning consulting, and steadycam services. In past, we've been fortunate enough to provide camera operators (ground or aerial) for several dozen features, television series, pilots, and Fortune 500 Companies including NBC, American Eagle Outfitters, Porsche, Autonation, Google, YouTube, Teles Properties, OnTrac, Acura.
